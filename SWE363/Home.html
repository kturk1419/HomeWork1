Saudi Arabia Information: The Kingdom of Saudi Arabia lies at the furthermost part of southwestern Asia.
It is bordered by the Arabian Gulf, United Arab Emirates and Qatar in the east; Red Sea in the west; Kuwait,
Iraq and Jordan in the north; Yemen and Oman in the south.

Explore Saudi Arabia On Google Map: Google Earth is a free
program from Google that allows you to explore satellite images showing
the cities and landscapes of Saudi Arabia and all of Asia in fantastic
detail. It works on your desktop computer, tablet, or mobile phone. The
images in many areas are detailed enough that you can see houses,
vehicles and even people on a city street. Google Earth is free and easy-
to-use.
